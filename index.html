Taylor Swift

Career

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter from Pennsylvania and made her debut in 2006 with her first single “Tim McGraw”. She began her career as a country singer during a time when country music was a male-dominated genre. In 2014, Taylor transitioned away from country music and released a pop music album titled “1989”. Taylor Swift is known for her unique storytelling and her incorporation of previous relationships into her music. Taylor Swift's fans easily connect with her relatable lyrics allowing her to grow a dedicated fanbase. Taylor Swift continues to consistently release music with her latest album titled “Midnights” released this year.
